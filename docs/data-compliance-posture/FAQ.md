
# Data Compliance Posture FAQ

## General Questions

**What is the Data Compliance Posture Dashboard?**
The Data Compliance Posture Dashboard provides a centralized view of your organization's compliance status across data governance, security, and regulatory requirements. It aggregates metrics from i-ARM to help you monitor, assess, and improve compliance management.

**Who should use this dashboard?**
Data governance teams, compliance officers, security administrators, and IT leadership responsible for maintaining regulatory compliance and data protection standards.

## Dashboard Features

**What metrics are included in the reports?**
Reports cover data classification, encryption status, access controls, audit logs, retention policies, and alignment with frameworks like GDPR, HIPAA, and SOC 2.

**How often is the dashboard updated?**
Data refreshes automatically based on your configuration, typically every 24 hours or on-demand to ensure current compliance visibility.

**Can I customize which metrics are displayed?**
Yes, you can configure widget selection, filters, and report parameters to match your organization's specific compliance requirements.

## Data and Reporting

**How do I export compliance reports?**
Use the export feature in the dashboard to generate PDF, CSV, or Excel reports for audit documentation and stakeholder communication.

**What is the difference between compliance status categories?**
Categories typically include: Compliant, At Risk, Non-Compliant, and Not Assessed. Each reflects the current state relative to your configured policies.

**How can I track compliance trends over time?**
Historical reports and trend analysis features allow you to compare metrics across reporting periods and identify patterns.

## Troubleshooting

**Why is data not appearing in my reports?**
Verify i-ARM data source connections, check user permissions, and ensure data collection is enabled for relevant systems.

**How do I contact support?**
Refer to your organization's IT support portal or compliance team for assistance with dashboard or i-ARM issues.
